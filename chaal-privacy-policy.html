1. Who we are

samvit.xyz ("we", "our", or "us") is the developer and provider of CHAAL (the "App"). CHAAL is a business-to-business (B2B) app used by construction companies to manage field teams, site attendance, and on-site operations.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how CHAAL collects, uses, and safeguards data. We act as a data processor/service provider for our customers and process data solely for operational purposes, as permitted by law.

3. Data we collect & why

A. Location Data

  • Purpose: To verify user attendance and allow managers to mark attendance for team members based on geofencing rules.
  • Use limitation: Location data is only used to verify if users and the people they mark attendance for are at the designated site. It is not used for analytics, tracking, or marketing.
  • Sharing: Not shared with third parties. Only accessible to the organization’s authorized managers and samvit.xyz support staff if required.

B. Camera & Photos

  • Purpose: Used to verify attendance and allow users to raise site queries, issues, and updates with photo proof.
  • Use limitation: Photos are stored securely and only used within the app for attendance and site-related purposes.
  • Sharing: Not shared externally or with third parties.

C. Internet Access

  • Purpose: Required to sync attendance, upload site data, and support updates between CHAAL and the customer’s server.
  • Use limitation: CHAAL does not include any advertising or analytics SDKs; internet use is limited to business functionality.

No Sale of Data: samvit.xyz does not sell or share user data for any commercial gain. All collected data is strictly used to support CHAAL’s intended business functionality.

4. How we use your data

  • To verify attendance using geofencing and photo validation.
  • To help managers track workforce presence at construction sites.
  • To enable site updates, issue reporting, and operational oversight.
  • To ensure system integrity, app functionality, and user authentication.

5. Sharing & transfers

  • CHAAL does not sell or share personal data with any external entities.
  • All information remains confidential within the business organization using the app.
  • Data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure under samvit.xyz’s management and not used for any unrelated purposes.

6. Retention

Data collected through CHAAL is retained only for as long as necessary to support attendance verification and business reporting, typically:

  • Attendance logs: 12 months
  • Photos and site updates: 12–24 months
  • Diagnostic logs: 90 days

Data is deleted or anonymized within 30 days after termination of services or customer request, unless legally required to retain it longer.

7. Security

  • All communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Data stored in encrypted databases with restricted access.
  • Regular audits and role-based access controls implemented.

8. Your rights & choices

As CHAAL is used in a B2B environment, individual user rights such as data correction or deletion can be requested through the employing organization or by contacting samvit.xyz at privacy@samvit.xyz.

9. Children

CHAAL is not intended for children under 16 years old and is used only by authorized business personnel.

10. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected here with a new effective date.
